My Games

  • Tic-Tac-Toe

    A classic game of strategy and wit, Tic-Tac-Toe is played on a 3x3 grid. Two players take turns marking empty cells with "X" and "O" in an attempt to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols. Quick and easy to play, Tic-Tac-Toe is a timeless game perfect for a quick mental challenge or a friendly competition

  • Rock, Paper, Scissors

    Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple yet entertaining hand game often used to make decisions or resolve disputes. Two players simultaneously choose one of three shapes with their hands: rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), or scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V). The winner is determined by the rules: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock.
